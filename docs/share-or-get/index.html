After making a mistake, for example at answering 2X4, it is not the table of 4 that is rehearsed more,
but "2 times something": 2X2, 2X3, 2X4 and 2X5, because the way of reasoning is alike. A certain way of reasoning is stimulated via hints (or via feedback in case of a mistake).

On mobile devices a keyboard with only numeric values is displayed automatically.		 Download
